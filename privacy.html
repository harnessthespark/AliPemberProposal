A gentle note

Your privacy

This is a temporary notice while a full privacy policy is prepared. It will be replaced soon with a complete version.

These tools are part of your work with your coach. They're here to help you notice and reflect — at your own pace, in your own words.

What you write

Notes and reflections you make in these tools are saved so you can return to them over time. You decide what to write, and what to leave out.

What's shared

Anything you choose to send using “Send to your coach” goes to your coach, and only when you choose to send it. Where your coach has set up a shared record, what you save may also be visible to them, to support your sessions — your coach will let you know if this applies to you.

Your choices

  • You're always free to write as much or as little as you like.
  • You can ask your coach what is kept, and ask for it to be removed.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, please speak to your coach. A full privacy notice — including who holds your information, the lawful basis for it, and how long it's kept — will follow.
