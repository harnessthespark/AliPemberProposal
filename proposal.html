What you'll see, and what you won't

By design, you don't see what your clients write between sessions. Their notes save privately to their own device — nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored anywhere you (or I) could access. The "Send to your coach" button gives them a one-tap way to share a snapshot with you when they choose. So you respond to what they bring to session, not what they did at 11pm on a Tuesday — which keeps the boundary clean and the GDPR picture trivially simple.

Looking ahead

Whichever route you choose now, these tools can later be folded into goodenoughmama.co.uk — as a member area, embedded modules, or a fuller client portal where you'd see your clients' patterns over time. That's a separate piece of work we'd scope when the time feels right.
